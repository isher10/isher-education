Namaste Dosto!

Hi, my name is Ishwar Beniwal from Nagaur, Rajasthan. I created Isher Education with one simple mission — to provide quality education to students who cannot afford expensive coaching institutes. Today, the internet gives us the power to learn anything and turn our dreams into reality. I noticed that many students don’t get proper notes and guidance at the right time, so I built this platform to solve that problem. Here you will find detailed notes on Botany, Zoology, and important NCERT topics, along with quizzes to test your preparation. Education should not be only about passing exams, but about truly understanding concepts. My goal is that you don’t just study here — you learn and grow. If this platform helps even a little in your success journey, that will be my biggest achievement. Keep learning, keep growing! 🚀
— Aapka Dost, Ishwar Beniwal
